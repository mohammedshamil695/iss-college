ISS Arts&Science College Perinthalmanna

.I S S Arts & Science College Perinthalmanna Ponniakursi started in 2012 under Islamic Service Society which is established 1982. Founded in year 2012, Iss Arts and Science College, Ponniakurissy is located in Malappuram, Kerala. It is a college affiliated to university approved by others. It is affiliated to Calicut University, Thenhipalem, Malapuram, Malappuram. The institute offers Under Graduate courses in various disciplines like Arts, Commerce, It & Computer, Management, and Science. It offers 6 courses across degrees like B.A., B.Com., B.C.A., B.B.A., B.Sc.PB No. 66, Ponniakurissy PO, Perinthalmanna, Kerala, Malappuram
